Get started

Download one of the wallets from the links above, and you’re all set to go! Alternatively, build your own from the source.

We would suggest staying away from online wallets, as the chance you’ll lose all of your coins is too high!

Get some coins

The easiest way to obtain coins is to buy them from an exchange. Catcoin is currently listed on:

  • CoinedUp: CAT/BTC, CAT/DOGE
  • Cryptsy: Please vote for our campaign!

If you have a powerful computer and want to mine coins yourself, join one of the following pools:

  • Solidpool P2P pool: 1% fee
  • p2pool.name: 1% fee
  • Luckyminers: 1.5% fee
  • Catpool.pw: 0% fee
  • TeamCatcoin: 0% fee
  • MintPool: 1% fee (0% for donors)
  • Bettercoin: 1% fee
  • Catpool.co: 1% fee
  • Coinium: 0% fee
  • Catpool.in: 0% fee
  • Cryptovalley: 1% fee
  • NonStopMine: 0% fee
